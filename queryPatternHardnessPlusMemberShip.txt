Querry Pattern  : Completeness


append (pattern 1, pattern2) : 
	<DecisionProblem name> and ( hasDescriptionLogic some ( DescriptionLogic and
	 (hasConstructors only ( ..or..or...)) and (hasAxiom only (..or..or..)) 
	 and (hasConstructor some...) and (hasConstructor some...) 
	 and (hasAxiom some ...) and (hasAxiom some ...) ) )



Example : 

ConceptSatisfiability and (hasDescriptionLogic some (DescriptionLogic 
 	and	(hasConstructor only 
		 (ConceptName or RoleComposition or Bottom or ExistentialRestriction or RoleIntersection
		 or RoleUnion or RoleName or UniversalRestriction or Complement or Top or Conjunction or
		 Disjunction)) 		
	and (hasAxiom only 
		(Inclusion or ConceptAssertion or Equivalence or RoleAssertion)) 
	and (hasConstructor  some ConceptName) 
	and (hasConstructor some RoleComposition)  and (hasConstructor some Bottom)  
	and (hasConstructor some ExistentialRestriction)  and (hasConstructor some RoleIntersection)  
	and (hasConstructor some RoleUnion)  and (hasConstructor some RoleName)  
	and (hasConstructor some UniversalRestriction)  and (hasConstructor some Complement)  
	and (hasConstructor some Top)  and (hasConstructor some Conjunction)  
	and (hasConstructor some Disjunction) 
	and (hasAxiom some Inclusion) and (hasAxiom some ConceptAssertion) 
	and (hasAxiom some Equivalence) and (hasAxiom some RoleAssertion))) 
	
	==> PSpace complete
